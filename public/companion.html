First Hike — Isskogel
Tuesday, September 1
Easy–Moderate

We take the Isskogelbahn gondola (FREE!) up to 1,900m. The hard climbing is done by the lift. Three options today:

🥇 Best choice: Summit + plateau loop. 5–7km, 2.5–3.5 hours. Views of the whole Zillertal.

🥈 If tired from travel: Just the summit (2,264m). 2 hours round trip.

🥉 If feeling super strong: Continue all the way to Kreuzjoch (2,558m). 6–7 hours.
