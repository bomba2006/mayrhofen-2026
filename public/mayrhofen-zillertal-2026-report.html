Ranking — By Steepness (Gain/km)

Why gain/km matters

Your Salzburg trip: comfortable at 33-50m/km. Total gain isn't the issue — steepness is. 750m over 18.5km (40m/km) = comfortable. 600m over 6.4km (94m/km) = a wall. Lifts cut the approach distance, so you can do long scenic walks at altitude with low gain.
