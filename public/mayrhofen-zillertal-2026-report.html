Why gain/km matters

Your Salzburg trip: comfortable at 33-50m/km. Total gain isn't the issue — steepness is. 750m over 18.5km (40m/km) = comfortable. 600m over 6.4km (94m/km) = a wall. Lifts cut the approach distance, so you can do long scenic walks at altitude with low gain.

Safety & Packing

All trails are well-marked and maintained. Gondolas skip the steepest valley approaches. Rain backup exists for every day. Pack: hiking boots, rain shell, layers (t-shirt + fleece + windbreaker), warm layer for glacier (0°C at 3,250m), daypack, sunscreen.
