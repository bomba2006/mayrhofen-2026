Ranking — By Steepness

Why gain/km matters

Total gain isn't the issue — steepness is. 750m over 18.5km (40m/km) = comfortable. 600m over 6.4km (94m/km) = a wall. Lifts cut approach distance so you do long scenic walks at altitude with low gain.
